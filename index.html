How to play

  • There are 4 bulbs in this game.
  • At the start, one bulb will blink.
  • Tap the button of the same colour that blinked to proceed.
  • If correct, the sequence will get longer: first 2 bulbs, then 3 bulbs, and so on.
  • Bulbs may repeat in the sequence, so pay close attention.
